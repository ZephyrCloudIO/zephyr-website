## Summary

This release adds email-verified open membership domains, tightens billing and subscription accuracy, and improves how teams manage tags, environments, and pinned versions. A range of reliability and UI fixes are included across the dashboard.

## Open Membership with Email Domain Verification

Organizations can now configure open membership domains so that anyone with a verified company email address can join automatically.

- Members who join through an open domain are assigned the correct role without manual approval.
- Organization admins can manage allowed domains from organization settings.
- Role assignments also update automatically when seats are downgraded, so access levels stay consistent with your subscription without manual intervention.

## Billing and Subscription Fixes

Several billing-related issues have been resolved to make subscription management more reliable.

- Billing history now loads correctly.
- Coupon discounts are applied consistently across the dashboard and email receipts.
- Coupon removal now works as expected.
- Payment method management has been improved with a clearer UI.

## Tag and Environment Naming Improvements

Renaming tags and environments is more reliable, and a few edge cases have been resolved.

- Tag and environment rename flows have been improved for accuracy and consistency.
- Names as short as two characters are now accepted for both environments and tags.
- Domain delimiter handling has been fixed to prevent formatting issues.

## Email Notifications Now Show Environment Context

Notification emails for non-production deployments now include the environment name in the subject line, making it easier to identify which environment triggered an alert without opening the email.

## Pinning and Navigation

- Pinning a version now shows a confirmation toast so you can tell the action was applied.
- Pin options are now paginated, improving performance when you have a large number of pinnable items.
- The sidebar has been improved for better navigation across the dashboard.

## Reliability Fixes

- Manual deploys now correctly respect version ownership policy.
- Retrying a failed deployment integration setup now properly re-runs the integration.
- Duplicate floating error notifications have been resolved.
- Usernames generated from email addresses now use the correct email part.

## What This Means for Teams

- Onboard teammates faster with email-verified open membership domains.
- More accurate billing history, coupon handling, and payment management.
- Fewer friction points when naming and renaming tags and environments.
- Clearer email alerts for non-production deployments.
